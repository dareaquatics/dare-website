Swim Groups


DARE Aquatics offers training and practice groups for swimmers of all ages and ability levels. Our goal is to provide age-specific training for all athletes, challenging and developing each individual to the best of their abilities. Please contact us to schedule a tryout or request more information.

Age Group 1

Coach: Andrew

Email: andrew[at]dareaquatics.com

Requirements:

  • Must know backstroke and freestyle
  • Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining

Group Description:

Pre Age Group Elite

Coach: Tevin

Email: tevin[at]dareaquatics.com

Requirements:

  • Must know all 4 strokes
  • Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining

Group Description:

  • Will be introduced to swim meets
  • Refining all four strokes (learning new drills / technique focus)
  • Will learn to work on turns, smooth transitions between strokes
  • 1 hour practice (dryland twice a week)
  • Must be 10 years old or older.
  • Must attend one swim meet per month.
  • Must be committed to 3 practices a week

Equipment Needed:

  • Kickboard
  • Fins
