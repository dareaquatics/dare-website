Swim Groups


DARE Aquatics offers training and practice groups for swimmers of all ages and ability levels. It is the goal of our team to offer age specific training for all of our athletes geared towards challenging and developing each individual to the best of THEIR abilities. Please email Linda Tang at linda_c_tang@yahoo.com to schedule a tryout or request for more information.

DARE Devils

Coach in charge: Coach Yvonne
Email: yvonne@dareaquatics.com

Requirements:
- Preferably water safe
- Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining
Group description:
- Will focus on water safety
- Will learn swim fundamentals
- Will learn back stroke and free style


Age Group 1

Coach in charge: Coach Andrew
Email: andrew@dareaquatics.com

Requirements:
- Must know back stroke and free style
- Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining
Group description:
- Will be introduced to basic swim team experience including swim meets
- Will refine back stroke & free style skills
- Will introduce breast stroke & butter fly kicks
- Must be committed to compete at least 2 swim meets (including Metro Committee Championships) per year


Pre Age Group Elite

Coach in charge: Coach Tevin
Email: tevin@dareaquatics.com

Requirements:
- Must know all 4 strokes
- Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining
Group description:
- Will be introduced to swim meets
- Refining all four strokes(learning new drills / technique focus)
- Will learn to work on turns, smooth transitions in between strokes
- 1 hour practice (dryland twice a week)
- Must be 10 years old or older.
- Must attend one swim meet per month.
- Must be committed to 3 practices a week
Equipment Needed
- Kickboard
- Fins


Age Group 2

Coach in charge: Coach Joseph
Email: joseph@dareaquatics.com

Requirements:
- Must have swim team / swim meet experience
- Must be committed to compete year round
- Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining
Group description:
- Will polish all 4 strokes
- Will learn proper turns and dive off the block and other necessary skill to compete
- Must be committed to compete at least 4 swim meets (Metro Champs mandatory)
- 1 hour daily wet training


Age Group Elite

Coach in charge: Coach Joseph

- Year-round training commitment
- Must meet age group championship meet standard
- Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining
Group description:
- 12 & Under championship group (JAG/WAG/JOs)
- 1.5 hr daily wet training 30 min dryland training (TBA)
