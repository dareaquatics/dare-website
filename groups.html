Swim Groups


DARE Aquatics offers training and practice groups for swimmers of all ages and ability levels. Our goal is to provide age-specific training for all athletes, challenging and developing each individual to the best of their abilities. Please contact us or email contact@dareaquatics.com to schedule a tryout or request more information.

DARE Devils

Coach: Yvonne

Email: yvonne@dareaquatics.com

Requirements:

  • Preferably water safe
  • Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining

Group Description:

  • Focus on water safety
  • Learn swim fundamentals
  • Learn backstroke and freestyle

Age Group 1

Coach: Andrew

Email: andrew@dareaquatics.com

Requirements:

  • Must know backstroke and freestyle
  • Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining

Group Description:

  • Will be introduced to basic swim team experience including swim meets
  • Will refine backstroke & freestyle skills
  • Will introduce breaststroke & butterfly kicks
  • Must be committed to compete at least 2 swim meets (including Metro Committee Championships) per year

Pre Age Group Elite

Coach: Tevin

Email: tevin@dareaquatics.com

Requirements:

  • Must know all 4 strokes
  • Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining

Group Description:

  • Will be introduced to swim meets
  • Refining all four strokes (learning new drills / technique focus)
  • Will learn to work on turns, smooth transitions between strokes
  • 1 hour practice (dryland twice a week)
  • Must be 10 years old or older.
  • Must attend one swim meet per month.
  • Must be committed to 3 practices a week

Equipment Needed:

  • Kickboard
  • Fins

Age Group 2

Coach: Joseph

Email: joseph@dareaquatics.com

Requirements:

  • Must have swim team / swim meet experience
  • Must be committed to compete year-round
  • Must hold current USA Swimming membership upon joining

Group Description:

  • Will polish all 4 strokes
  • Will learn proper turns and dive off the block and other necessary skills to compete
  • Must be committed to compete at least 4 swim meets (Metro Champs mandatory)
  • 1 hour daily wet training
