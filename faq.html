General Information

What is club swimming?
Club swimming is a competitive swimming program for swimmers of all ages and abilities. It offers structured training, regular competitions, and the opportunity to develop swimming skills in a team environment.
What are the benefits of joining a club swimming team?
Benefits include professional coaching, improved swimming skills, increased fitness, personal discipline, teamwork, and the chance to compete at various levels. Swimmers also make lasting friendships and learn valuable life skills.
Where is the club located?
Our club operates out of several pools in the Alhambra area, including Alhambra Park Pool and Granada Pool. Please check our Facebook group for specific pool locations and training schedules.

Registration and Membership

How do I register my child for the team?
You can schedule a tryout or visit us during practice hours at any of our pool locations. Our coaches will guide you through the registration process, including filling out necessary forms and paying the membership fees.
What are the membership fees?
Membership fees vary based on the training group and competition level. We offer monthly payment options. Please contact us for more detailed information about our current fee structure.
Is there a trial period before committing to the team?
Yes, we usually offer a one-week tryout period where your child can participate in practice sessions to see if club swimming is a good fit. Contact us to arrange a trial session.

Training and Practice

What is the training schedule like?
Our training schedule varies by group level. Generally, practices are held 6 days a week (Monday to Saturday), with sessions that are usually 2 hours. Detailed schedules for each group are available on our Facebook group.
What equipment is needed for practices?
Swimmers should have a swimsuit, goggles, swim cap, and a water bottle. Coaches may also recommend additional equipment such as fins, kickboards, snorkels, and paddles, which can be purchased from Amazon or any other sporting goods retailer.
How are training groups determined?
Training groups are based on age, skill level, and experience. New swimmers will undergo an evaluation by our coaches to determine the most appropriate group placement. We regularly assess swimmers' progress and may suggest group changes as they improve.

Competitions

How often are swim meets held?
Swim meets are typically held once or twice a month. The schedule includes local, regional, and occasionally national competitions. Swimmers are encouraged to participate in meets appropriate to their skill level.

Are there additional fees for swim meets?
Yes, there are usually entry fees for swim meets, which vary depending on the competition level and the number of events entered. These fees are generally paid by the parents. We strive to keep these costs reasonable and provide advance notice of all meet-related expenses.
What is the role of parents at swim meets?
Parents are essential supporters at swim meets. They help with transportation, provide encouragement, and may volunteer for various roles such as timing or officiating. Parent involvement is crucial for the success of the team and enhances the overall experience for all swimmers.

Coaching and Support

Who are the coaches?
Our coaching staff consists of experienced and certified coaches who are passionate about swimming and dedicated to the development of our athletes. You can find detailed information about our coaches on the Coaches page.
How can parents communicate with coaches?
Parents can communicate with coaches via email or during designated times before or after practices. You may also contact them via their email, which is provided on the Swim Groups page. We encourage open communication to ensure the best experience for your swimmer.

Additional Information

What is the club's policy on swimmer behavior and conduct?
We expect all swimmers to demonstrate respect, sportsmanship, and commitment. Our code of conduct outlines specific expectations and consequences for inappropriate behavior. This policy is reviewed during registration and is available on our Team Policy Documents page.
How can parents stay informed about club activities and updates?
We communicate through email newsletters, our website, and social media channels, such as Instagram or Facebook. We encourage parents to regularly check these platforms for the most up-to-date information.
How can I get more involved with the club?
We welcome parent volunteers for various roles, including meet organization, fundraising, and social events. Your involvement is highly valued and helps enhance the experience for all swimmers. Please speak with a coach or board member about volunteer opportunities.
