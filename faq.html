What are the benefits of swimming?

Swimming offers a range of wholesome benefits that cater to individuals at various stages of skill development, making it a sport cherished by both parents and children alike. We believe that swimming presents core advantages including:

  • Enjoyment: Whether it's the thrill of achieving personal bests or engaging in friendly card games during swim meets, the inherent fun of swimming keeps participants engaged and committed to the sport.
  • Fitness: From instilling water safety in children to providing fundamental aerobic exercise for all ages, swimming offers a low-impact workout that promotes lifelong fitness principles and keeps individuals in excellent shape.
  • Friendships: A significant aspect of swimming is the friendships forged through shared experiences, whether it's cheering each other on during races, socializing between heats, or motivating one another to push a little harder during practice sessions.
What is the practice schedule?

The swimming season starts in mid-August and lasts until the end of July, spanning virtually the entire year. Practices are held Monday to Friday, running from 5 PM to 7 PM, and on Saturdays from 7 AM to 9 AM. Unless there is a risk of lightning, practices proceed even in rainy conditions. Dryland is usually held before practice, and while rainy weather may lead to the cancellation of dryland, regular practice continues as scheduled.

Is the pool crowded?

Certainly not. We pride ourselves on maintaining exceptional swimmer-to-coach ratios. Here, you won't merely blend into the crowd; our focus ensures that every swimmer receives individual attention. Our dedicated coaches and parents prioritize the well-being of each swimmer, striving to maintain a healthy balance and provide the support necessary for their development.

Is the pool outdoor and is it heated for the winter months?

Yes, as with most pools in Southern California, the pool is outdoor and is heated at about 80 degrees throughout the year.

How do I join the team? Are there tryouts?

Yes, there are tryouts. Please contact us for more information.
