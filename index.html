What is DARE Aquatics?


DARE Aquatics is a dedicated competitive swim team that's all about the thrill of racing in the water. We're a close-knit group who are passionate about improving our skills and achieving our best in the pool. Our team offers a supportive and encouraging environment for swimmers of all ages and levels, from beginners looking to refine their strokes to experienced athletes with dreams of reaching the highest levels of competition. With experienced coaches and a focus on technique, endurance, and teamwork, we're committed to helping our members excel in the exciting world of competitive swimming.
