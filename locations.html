Why Choose DARE Aquatics Facilities?

Our training locations are carefully selected and maintained to provide the best possible environment for our swimmers. Here's what sets our facilities apart:

  • With features like adjustable lane lines, starting blocks, and dry-land training areas, our facilities cater to all aspects of swim training.
  • On-site lifeguards, appropriate pool depths, and well-maintained equipment ensure a safe training environment for all our swimmers.
  • Visible pace clocks help swimmers and coaches monitor progress and performance accurately.
  • With nearby parking, accessible restrooms, and water fountains, we ensure comfort for both swimmers and their families.
  • Our facilities are designed to support training throughout the year, helping our swimmers maintain consistency in their routines.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced competitive swimmer, our facilities provide the ideal setting for you to develop your skills, improve your technique, and achieve your swimming goals.


DARE Aquatics utilizes two pool locations to optimize our training programs and ensure the best possible experience for our swimmers. By splitting our practice sessions between these two facilities, we're able to:

  • Maintain smaller group sizes for more personalized instruction
  • Offer a variety of practice times to accommodate different schedules
  • Provide diverse training environments to enhance skill development
  • Ensure adequate lane space for swimmers of all levels

If you're scheduling a tryout or have questions about which location you should attend, please reach out to us via contact form OR email us at contact@dareaquatics.com
