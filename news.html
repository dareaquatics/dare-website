2024 Clothing Donation Drive

Author: Joy Lim

Published on October 14, 2024

Clothing Donation Drive - Help Us Make a Difference

Dear Parents and Swimmers!
It's that time again! We're organizing a Clothing Drive to collect wearable clothes, shoes, backpacks, undergarments, and purses. This is an excellent opportunity to declutter your closet and contribute to our team's fundraiser!
Here are the key details:
- What we are collecting: Wearable clothes, shoes, backpacks, undergarments, and purses.
- We are NOT accepting towels, stuffed animals, toys, or torn or stained clothes.
- Only use 13-gallon bag
**Collection Date** 1 DAY ONLY on Friday, October 25, 2024, at the pool (Location, TBD), so please plan your drop-off accordingly.
We need parent volunteers to help sort, collect, and load items into the trailer on the collection day. And we need volunteers to unload the trailer and load the collector’s truck the following day.
