Training Programs

Groups for every level

From beginners to national-level competitors — we have a group tailored for your swimmer.

Beginner

DARE Devils

Entry-level group focusing on water safety, freestyle, and backstroke fundamentals.

Intermediate

Age Group 1 & 2

Competitive development with all four strokes, meets, and technique refinement.

Advanced

Age Group Elite

Championship-level training for 12 & under JAG/WAG/JOs competitors.

Elite

Pre-National & National

Sectionals and above training. College prep and elite competitive development.
